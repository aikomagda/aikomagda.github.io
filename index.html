FAQs

How do I keep aware of 3.14 chan events?
At the bottom of our page we have a form you can fill in to sign up for our monthly newsletter.

How do I post my questions?
You need a premium account to post questions. If you don't have one, you're limited to viewing other's questions.

How do I contact staff?
You can contact us using our email addresses in the "Our Staff" section of the website.

The website keeps crashing - what do I do?
Unfortunately this website was written in Repl.it which is prone to crashing and there is not much we can do at the moment.
